# solo — no teammates, just careful thinking

No teammates this session. Work alone.

Use this preset when the task is small enough that coordination overhead would dominate, or when you want to avoid the ~N× token cost of spawning a team. The bridge, scrollback, tmux layout, and CLAUDE.md sync all still apply — you just don't have a team roster.

You can always spawn teammates later in the same session by asking explicitly:

> spawn a teammate as a reviewer to look at what I've done so far
